No Woman No Cry

Description:

Reflects on Bob Marley's time growing up in Trenchtown, Jamaica. He sings about the rough time he had growing up and how the struggles shaped him as a person.

Themes

Hope & Resilience, Comfort, Community

Impact

  • Live recording more popular than studio version
  • Originally credited to a friend of Marley
  • An important influence that helped make Reggae a more mainstream music genre
