Take Me Home Country Roads

Description:

This song tells the story of someone who wants to make it back to a place in the country that is away from modern city life. They talk about Virginia as 'Mountain Mama' signifying the comfort that they feel when they are driving through the old dirt roads there.

Themes

Home, nostalgia, comfort, rural beauty

Impact

  • Reached #2 Billboard top 100 in 1971
  • One of John Denvers Signature Songs
  • Still popular to this day most often at sporting events
